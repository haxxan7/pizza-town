About Pizza Town

Pizza Town Bahawalpur is a popular fast-food spot located at SS Tower, Noor Mahal Road, near Imtiaz Mega, Sadiq Colony, Bahawalpur. They offer a wide range of pizzas, burgers, fried chicken, and combo deals for families. The restaurant provides dine-in, takeaway, and home delivery services. It's open daily from 11:00 AM to 2:00 AM (opens at 9:00 AM on weekends). You can contact them at (062) 2880500 or 0304-5400010. Pizza Town is well-known for its quality food and friendly atmosphere.
